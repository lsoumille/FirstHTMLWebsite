History

The movie theater Metropolis has been created in 1990 inside the city of Metropolis. It's now a twelve-screen cinema, whereas there was only one at the beginning.
It is mainly famous because of the many premieres it hosted.
It's also the main place where the famous Metropolis festival happens.
